Cafe on campus:

  • Smart Cafe

      Feed your brain. Smart cafe features organic and locally grown foods that are good for your brain. All our food comes from farms that are within 20 miles of campus. All our eggs come from cage-free chickens which means higher levels of choline for your diet. Choline has been scientifically proven to boost brain power and memory retention.

      Our menu is available for ordering online with vegan and gluten-free options clearly labeled making conscious eating easy.

  • Rooftop Garden Cafe

      The Rooftop Garden Cafe is one of the most unique eating experiences on campus. All our fruits and vegetables are grown on one of the nine roofs designated to us on campus, and all our meats come from Monterey County. This makes us the most locally-grown cafe on campus.

      Foods that are not subjected to long transportation and storage times are more nutritionally packed and reduces the carbon footprint by 90% compared to crops that must be shipped across the country. Tour our rooftop gardens on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 3-4 pm.
